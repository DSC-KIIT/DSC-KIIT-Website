Developer Student Club

Google colludes with academy students who are enthusiastic about growing developer communities and supports them with commencing student clubs on their campuses. Developer Student Clubs is a program that recognizes and supports university students who are excited about growing developer communities that cultivate learning, sharing, and collaboration.
