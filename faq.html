GCP Crash Course FAQ

How to take up the crash course?

We have provided instructions and links to each tracks on the home page. Refer to them.

What is Qwiklabs?

Qwiklabs is a platform where you can learn various Cloud technologies on GCP and AWS.

Do I need to pay or buy anything?

No, the course is free and you are provided with an Advantage Subscription for a month on Qwiklabs.

Do I need an account on Google Cloud Platform to take up the course?

Not at all. You can login to Qwiklabs with your Google Account and for each lab you get temporary access to GCP.

Is there a time limit for the Crash Course?

You can take up and complete the tracks anytime before October 31.

How many tracks should I complete to get goodies?

Atleast one. But don't do it for the sake of goodies. :)

I completed one track, what should I do?

You may or may not learn another track (Up to you) but fill the task completion form.

What are other benefits I get?

Upon completing each quest you get a digital badge, certifying you that you are proficient in that domain of GCP.

What is a public profile in Qwiklabs and where to find it?

Everyone on qwiklabs will have a public profile. You can find it under My Learnings. Under this page in the right top side corner you will find "View Public profile" For first time it will ask you to create a public profile. The URL you get is public and anyone can see your digital badges here. You need to submit this link in the completion form.

When and where to do this course?

Your home, park, college are few places we could suggest. Free times, boring lectures, holidays, weekdays, anytime! Learn and complete at your pace :)
